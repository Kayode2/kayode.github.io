Diabetes Prediction in Pima Indian Women

In order to diagnose whether a patient has diabetes, the goal of this research is to study and analyze a dataset that was originally obtained from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases (NIDDK). The dataset consists of a single goal-dependent variable called "Outcome" and a number of independent medical predictor factors. Notably, every patient in this dataset is a female with a Pima Indian origin who is at least 21 years old. This dataset offers an excellent chance to use data science and machine learning methods to enhance the quality of healthcare for this particular group.
