Employee Attrition

The project, titled “HR Attrition,” focuses on investigating the decrease in employee numbers within the company caused by resignations, retirements, or deaths. By utilizing different metrics, the project provides in-depth insights into the attrition rate. Exploratory Data Analysis, a crucial step in gaining insights from data, was effectively conducted using the versatile tool of Excel. The process involved meticulous attention to detail, including the meticulous removal of null and duplicate values. By eliminating these inconsistencies, the reliability and accuracy of our analysis were significantly enhanced. Visualization was carried out finally in PowerBI

Apple’s Journey towards carbon
neutrality by 2030

In 2020, after announcing their corporate operations were officially carbon neutral, Apple pledged to make their products carbon neutral by 2030. To achieve this goal, they set their emissions for 2015 (38.4 million metric tons CO2e) as the baseline and will aim to reduce them by 75% by 2030. The remaining 25% of gross emissions (9.6 million metric tons CO2e) will be removed using carbon offsets, bringing the net emissions to 0.
